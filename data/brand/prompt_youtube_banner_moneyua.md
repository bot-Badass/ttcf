# ПРОМПТ ДЛЯ ГЕНЕРАЦІЇ YOUTUBE BANNER
# Канал: MoneyUA — фінансова грамотність для українців
# Модель: DALL-E 3 / GPT-4o / Midjourney
# =====================================================
# ІНСТРУКЦІЯ: Скопіюй текст нижче і вклей у ChatGPT (з увімкненим DALL-E)
# або в Midjourney як /imagine prompt
# =====================================================

Create a professional YouTube channel banner for a Ukrainian personal finance education channel called "MONEY UA".

## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
- Canvas size: 2560 × 1440 pixels, aspect ratio 16:9
- Safe zone (visible on ALL devices including mobile): centered 1546 × 423 px area
- All critical text and logo elements MUST be within the safe zone
- File format: PNG, sRGB color space

## BRAND IDENTITY
- Channel name: MONEY UA
- Tagline: Фінанси простою мовою
- Niche: Personal finance education for Ukrainians — budgets, credits, deposits, taxes, fraud protection
- Tone: Professional, trustworthy, approachable — NOT corporate or cold
- Target audience: Ordinary Ukrainians aged 20–45, not investors or bankers

## COLOR PALETTE (use EXACTLY these hex values)
- Primary background: #0d1b3e (deep navy blue)
- Accent / highlight: #FFD700 (gold)
- Secondary background: #1a3a6e (medium navy)
- Text: #FFFFFF (white) for headlines, #FFD700 for channel name
- Subtle gradient allowed: dark navy left → slightly lighter navy right

## SAFE ZONE CONTENT (center 1546×423 px — critical elements here)
1. Channel name "MONEY UA" — large, bold, gold (#FFD700), left or center aligned
2. Tagline "Фінанси простою мовою" — smaller white text below channel name
3. A gold horizontal accent line or decorative element separating name and tagline
4. Upload schedule badge (right side of safe zone): "Нові відео щотижня"
5. A small coin/money icon or minimalist chart icon next to the channel name

## OUTER CANVAS (visible on desktop and TV — decorative only)
- Left side: subtle abstract geometric pattern — thin gold diagonal lines or hexagonal grid on dark navy
- Right side: a stylized illustration of stacked coins, a rising bar chart, or a credit card — all in gold/white line art style on dark navy
- Top and bottom edges: thin gold accent bars (full width, 10–15 px)
- Background: smooth dark navy gradient, not flat — very subtle depth

## TYPOGRAPHY STYLE
- Channel name font style: Bold, modern sans-serif (similar to Montserrat ExtraBold or Bebas Neue)
- Tagline font style: Regular weight, clean sans-serif, smaller size
- NO decorative or script fonts — must be readable on mobile
- All text has subtle dark shadow or outline for readability on any background

## DESIGN RULES
- Minimalist and clean — no clutter, maximum 4–5 visual elements total
- NO stock photography of people
- NO flags or political symbols
- High contrast between text and background (WCAG AA minimum)
- Profile picture circle overlaps bottom-left corner on desktop — do NOT place key elements there
- The design must look equally good when cropped to just the 1546×423 safe zone on mobile

## MOOD REFERENCES
- Think: Bloomberg professional meets modern Ukrainian fintech brand
- Similar energy to: Wise, monobank, or N26 brand aesthetics
- Clean, trustworthy, modern — not flashy or crypto-bro

## OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS
Generate the banner at maximum available resolution. The final result must look sharp and professional at both the full 2560×1440 size (TV/desktop) and when cropped to the 1546×423 mobile safe zone.
