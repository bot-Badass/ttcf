Create a professional social media profile picture (avatar / channel icon) for a Ukrainian personal finance education brand called "MONEY UA".

## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
- Upload size: 720 × 720 pixels, 1:1 square aspect ratio
- Display: shown as a CIRCLE on TikTok and Instagram — all critical elements must be centered and within the inner circle
- Safe circle: treat the inner 640×640 px area as the visible zone — keep 40px margin from all edges
- File format: PNG with transparent background preferred, or solid dark background
- Color space: sRGB

## BRAND IDENTITY
- Brand name: MONEY UA
- Niche: Personal finance education for ordinary Ukrainians
- Tone: Modern, clean, trustworthy — not corporate, not crypto, not aggressive

## COLOR PALETTE (match the YouTube banner)
- Primary: #0d1b3e (deep navy blue) — use as background
- Accent: #FFD700 (gold) — use for main icon/symbol and text
- Text: #FFFFFF (white) — for secondary text if needed
- No other colors — maximum 3 colors total

## VISUAL CONCEPT (choose the strongest option)
Design a bold, minimalist logo-style icon. One of these concepts:

OPTION A — Letter mark with icon:
- Bold letters "M₴" (M + Ukrainian hryvnia symbol) in gold on dark navy
- Clean, geometric letterform — similar to a bank logo
- The ₴ symbol cleverly integrated into or next to the M
- No decorative elements — pure typography

OPTION B — Coin icon:
- A stylized gold coin on dark navy background
- The coin face shows "M UA" or a simple rising arrow / bar chart
- Minimal detail — must be readable at 40×40px
- Clean circular frame with thin gold border

OPTION C — Shield + letter:
- A geometric shield shape in gold on navy
- Bold letter "M" or "₴" inside the shield
- Conveys: trust, protection, financial security

## TYPOGRAPHY (if text is included)
- Maximum 3–4 characters visible (M UA or ₴ or M₴)
- Font style: Bold geometric sans-serif, NOT handwritten or script
- Text must be readable when the icon is displayed at 40×40 px (TikTok comment size)

## DESIGN RULES
- Maximum simplicity — this must work at 40×40 px (tiny comment icon) AND at 200×200 px
- No gradients on the main icon element — flat or with very subtle gold metallic sheen
- No photographs of people
- No complex illustrations with fine lines that disappear at small sizes
- Strong contrast between icon and background
- The icon should look equally good on white, dark, and colorful backgrounds (since platforms use different UI themes)
- Think: app icon quality — clean enough to be a fintech app icon

## PLATFORM DISPLAY SIZES FOR REFERENCE
- TikTok profile page: 200×200 px (shown as circle)
- TikTok video feed overlay: ~40×40 px (shown as circle — must still be recognizable)
- Instagram profile: 150×150 px (shown as circle)
- YouTube channel icon: 98×98 px on desktop (shown as circle)
- YouTube channel icon: 48×48 px in comments (shown as circle)

## MOOD REFERENCES
- Similar visual language to: N26 (bank), Wise, Revolut, monobank
- NOT similar to: crypto logos, gambling brands, pyramid scheme aesthetics
- Clean, modern European fintech energy

## OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS
Generate at 720×720 pixels. The icon must be immediately recognizable and brand-consistent across all three platforms when displayed at sizes ranging from 40px to 200px.

## VARIATION REQUEST
After the main icon, generate 2 alternative color variations:
1. White background with navy/gold icon (for use on light backgrounds)
2. Gold background with navy icon (accent version for stories/highlights)
